What we do

Software Development with C++ and Java

At DLK Agency, we specialize in high-performance software development using C++ and Java. Our expertise covers a wide range of applications, from enterprise solutions to real-time systems requiring robust and efficient code. We follow industry best practices, ensuring scalability, maintainability, and optimal performance in every project. Whether you need a custom desktop application, back-end services, or system-level programming, our team delivers high-quality, production-ready software tailored to your needs.

AI Model Design with Python

Artificial Intelligence is at the core of modern innovation, and we excel in designing advanced Deep Learning models using Python, leveraging frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch. Our expertise spans computer vision, and predictive analytics. We focus on building optimized, well-trained models that drive intelligent decision-making, whether for automation, pattern recognition, or business intelligence applications. From dataset preparation to model deployment, we ensure end-to-end AI solutions that integrate seamlessly into your ecosystem

Device Integration & Embedded Development

Our team has extensive experience in device integration and embedded systems development, working with C, cross-compilers, and Microchip components. We design firmware and low-level software for embedded applications, ensuring seamless communication between hardware and software components. Whether it’s interfacing with IoT devices, industrial machines, or smart sensors, we develop highly efficient embedded solutions that maximize performance and reliability. From driver development to real-time system programming, we provide robust embedded solutions for mission-critical applications.
